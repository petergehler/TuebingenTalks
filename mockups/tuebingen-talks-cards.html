Avatars as Interfaces: Shaping Experience and the Body in Mixed Reality

Prof. Niels Henze, University of Tübingen

Mixed reality allows us to change not only what users see, but also how they perceive their environment and their own bodies. This talk treats avatars as interactive interfaces rather than mere visual representations, asking what becomes possible when the user's body itself becomes part of the interface.
